## Intro to Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is just on-demand computing resources delivered to you or your business via the internet.

Put roughly, a cloud is a series of computers that exist in a data center. These computers are strung together to create some sort of network of services. Resources are efficiently pooled together over a virtual infrastructure.

Basically, the cloud is just somebody else's servers.
